## 两整数之和

**原链接**：<https://leetcode-cn.com/problems/sum-of-two-integers/>

#### 位运算[Bit Manipulation]    

##### 难度：**`Easy`**

----- 
<p>Calculate the sum of two integers <i>a</i> and <i>b</i>, but you are <b>not allowed</b> to use the operator <code>+</code> and <code>-</code>.</p>

<div>

<pre>

</pre>

<div>
